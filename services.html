Services

Couples

“The highest function of love is that it makes the loved one a unique and irreplaceable being.” ― Tom Robbins

I provide Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) to couples in distress. Research indicates that about 70% of couples who receive EFT significantly improve their outcomes and reduce their distress. EFT focuses on developing closeness and connection once again in couples who may have lost it. In addition, I will help increase your communication skills in order to develop empathy for one another and the ability to discuss your emotions without the discussion leading to a battle.

Individuals

I have experience working with suicidal ideation, domestic violence, grief, anxiety, multicultural issues, and depression. I have been trained in evidence-based practices for OCD as well as PTSD. In addition, I am competent in working with children and teenagers suffering from various symptoms of distress.
